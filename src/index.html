DELAYED FLIGHT COMPENSATION

How much can you get?

If your flight has been delayed and as a result, you arrive at your final destination three hours late or more, then you can claim monetary compensation from the airline. The amount is prescribed by EU261 Regulation and is determinded by the distanc between the departure and arrival airports.

$250
For flights up to 1.500km
$400
For flights between 1.500 and 3500km
$600
For flights over 3.500km
